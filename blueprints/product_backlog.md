# Product Backlog: SGI Seaflow

## Epics & User Stories

### 1. Normativo (Legal Compliance)
*   **US-1.1: Visualización de Normativa Aplicable**
    *   **As a** User, **I want to** see a list of all regulations that apply to my operation, **so that** I can ensure compliance.
    *   **Acceptance Criteria:** Table with Filter by Area, Name, and Status.
*   **US-1.2: Gestión de Artículos**
    *   **As a** Compliance Officer, **I want to** associate specific articles with a regulation, **so that** I can track requirements at a granular level.
*   **US-1.3: Alertas de Actualización**
    *   **As a** User, **I want to** receive notifications when a regulation is updated, **so that** I don't miss new legal requirements.

### 2. Certificaciones (Certifications)
*   **US-2.1: Seguimiento de Sellos (ASC/BAP)**
    *   **As a** Manager, **I want to** see the current status of environmental and quality certifications, **so that** I can maintain market access.
*   **US-2.2: Repositorio de Certificados**
    *   **As a** Auditor, **I want to** download valid certificate documents from the platform.

### 3. Permisología (Permits & Concessions)
*   **US-3.1: Dashboard de Concesiones**
    *   **As a** User, **I want to** see a consolidated view of maritime and land concessions, **so that** I can track renewals.
*   **US-3.2: Alertas de Vencimiento de Permisos**
    *   **As a** Legal Representative, **I want to** get notified 60 days before a permit expires.

### 4. Capatitaciones (Training)
*   **US-4.1: Matriz de Capacitación**
    *   **As a** HR/HSE Officer, **I want to** view a matrix of completed vs. required training for all staff, **so that** I can plan training sessions.
*   **US-4.2: Registro de Asistencia**
    *   **As a** Trainer, **I want to** upload attendance lists for training modules with evidence files.

### 5. Auditorías (Audits)
*   **US-5.1: Planificación de Auditorías**
    *   **As a** Quality Manager, **I want to** schedule internal and external audits for different areas (Caucahue, Pullao, Chequian).
*   **US-5.2: Registro de Hallazgos (Interna/Cert/Norm)**
    *   **As a** Auditor, **I want to** record findings during audits with status (Cumple/No Cumple) and associate them with specific requirements.
*   **US-5.3: Seguimiento de No Conformidades**
    *   **As a** Responsible Party, **I want to** upload evidence and close findings before the due date.

### 6. INFAS (Environmental Reports)
*   **US-6.1: Repositorio Histórico INFA**
    *   **As a** Environmental Specialist, **I want to** access historical INFA reports (2008-2023) and compare results.
*   **US-6.2: Registro de Parámetros Químicos**
    *   **As a** Specialist, **I want to** log and visualize PH, Redox, and M.O. levels per station.

### 7. Bitácora (Communication & Activities)
*   **US-7.1: Gestión de Cartas (Entrada/Salida)**
    *   **As a** Admin, **I want to** track official correspondence with entities (SEA, SSFFAA, SMA) with autoincremental correlatives.
*   **US-7.2: Registro de Actividades Operacionales**
    *   **As a** User, **I want to** log specific activities (Filmatch, Limpieza, Mantenimiento) linked to compliance categories.

### 8. Reportes (Reporting Dashboard)
*   **US-8.1: Dashboard de Reportes Externos**
    *   **As a** Manager, **I want to** see a list of external reports required by entities (SINADER, SIDREP) and their deadlines.
*   **US-8.2: Carta Gantt de Reportes**
    *   **As a** User, **I want to** see a monthly Gantt view of report compliance (Realizado/Pendiente).
